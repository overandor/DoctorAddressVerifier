# Privacy Policy - Doctor Address Verifier

**Last Updated:** June 13, 2026

## Overview
Doctor Address Verifier is a macOS application designed to verify healthcare provider addresses against publicly available government databases and healthcare directories. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection
**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### What the App Does
- Processes physician name and address information you provide
- Queries publicly available databases (NPPES, Medicare.gov, State License Boards, healthcare directories)
- Returns verification results to your local device
- All processing happens locally on your Mac

### Data We Process
- **Physician names and addresses** you input for verification
- This data is processed locally and not stored
- No personal health information (PHI) is collected or transmitted

## Data Storage
**No data is stored on our servers.**

- All verification results are stored locally on your device
- You can export results to CSV for your own records
- We have no access to your data
- No cloud synchronization or backup

## Third-Party Services
The app queries publicly available databases:
- **NPPES** (National Plan and Provider Enumeration System) - US government database
- **Medicare.gov** - CMS public data
- **State Medical License Boards** - Public license verification
- **Healthcare directories** (Healthgrades, WebMD, Zocdoc, etc.) - Public provider listings

These services are accessed via their public APIs or websites. We do not share your data with third parties beyond what is necessary to query these public databases.

## Data Security
- All processing happens on your local device
- The app runs in macOS sandbox with restricted permissions
- Network access is limited to querying public databases
- No encryption keys or credentials are stored in the app

## Your Rights
- You control what data you input into the app
- You can delete the app and all associated data at any time
- Exported CSV files are stored in your chosen location
- No account registration or personal information required

## Children's Privacy
This app is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the date at the top of this policy.
