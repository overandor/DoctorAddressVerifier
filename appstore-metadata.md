## Description (Full)
Doctor Address Verifier is a powerful tool for healthcare organizations, medical recruiters, and insurance companies to verify physician addresses against authoritative government databases and healthcare directories.

**Key Features:**
- Multi-source verification across 11 data sources including NPPES, Medicare.gov, State License Boards, Healthgrades, WebMD, Zocdoc, and more
- Real-time address standardization and validation
- National Provider Identifier (NPI) lookup and verification
- Confidence scoring with evidence tracking
- Batch processing for large physician lists
- Export results to CSV for further analysis

**Data Sources:**
- NPPES (National Plan and Provider Enumeration System) - Official US government database
- Medicare.gov Physician Compare
- State Medical License Boards
- Healthgrades, Vitals, WebMD, Zocdoc, RateMDs
- CMS Open Payments data
- Hospital affiliation databases

**Use Cases:**
- Verify physician addresses before outreach campaigns
- Validate provider data for credentialing
- Clean up physician databases for insurance networks
- Ensure accurate provider information for patient directories

**Privacy & Security:**
- All data processing happens locally on your Mac
- No personal health information is stored or transmitted
- Uses only publicly available provider databases
- Sandbox-compliant with network client access

Perfect for medical groups, hospitals, insurance companies, and healthcare recruiters who need accurate, verified physician contact information.
